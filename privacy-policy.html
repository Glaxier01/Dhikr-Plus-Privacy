Privacy Policy for Dhikr+

Last updated: March 2026

Dhikr+ (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices when using the App.

Dhikr+ is a digital tasbih and dhikr counter designed to help users keep track of dhikr sessions and personal remembrance practices.

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Information

Dhikr+ does not require account creation or login.

The App does not collect or request:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Addresses
  • User accounts
  • Personal identifiers

The developer does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All user-generated data is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to the developer or any external server.

Local data is stored using SQLite databases and Android SharedPreferences.

The following information may be stored locally:

Dhikr Session History

  • Dhikr name
  • Count
  • Duration
  • Timestamp

Custom Dhikrs

  • User-created dhikr names
  • Custom round targets

App Settings

  • Sound settings
  • Vibration settings
  • Theme preferences
  • Language preferences

This information remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to the developer.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data
  • Performance and diagnostics data

This data is collected directly by Google and processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

The developer does not have access to this data.

4. GDPR Consent

For users in regions where privacy regulations apply (such as the European Economic Area), the App may display a consent form at startup.

Users can choose:

  • Personalized ads
  • Non-personalized ads

Your choice determines how ads may be tailored.

5. Permissions Used

INTERNET

Used only to load advertisements from Google AdMob.

VIBRATE

Used to provide haptic feedback when counting dhikr.

The App does not access:

  • Contacts
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Files or media
  • Location services

6. Analytics and Tracking

Dhikr+ does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Firebase analytics
  • Behavioral tracking

7. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

The only third-party service used is Google AdMob for advertising.

8. Children's Privacy

Dhikr+ is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device.

You can delete all stored data by:

  • Clearing the app storage from Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app

10. Data Security

Since Dhikr+ does not collect personal data, no personal data is stored on external servers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
