Privacy Policy for Dhikr+ Last updated: March 2026 Dhikr+ (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices when using the App. Dhikr+ is a digital tasbih and dhikr counter designed to help users keep track of dhikr sessions and personal remembrance practices. 1. Information We Collect No Personal Information Dhikr+ does not require account creation or login. The App does not collect or request: Names Email addresses Phone numbers Addresses User accounts Personal identifiers The developer does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device All user-generated data is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to the developer or any external server. Local data is stored using SQLite databases and Android SharedPreferences. The following information may be stored locally: Dhikr Session History Dhikr name Count Duration Timestamp Custom Dhikrs User-created dhikr names Custom round targets App Settings Sound settings Vibration settings Theme preferences Language preferences This information remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to the developer. 3. Advertising (Google AdMob) The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device, including: Device information Advertising ID IP address Approximate location Ad interaction data Performance and diagnostics data This data is collected directly by Google and processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy The developer does not have access to this data and does not control how Google processes it. 4. GDPR Consent (EEA and Applicable Regions) For users in regions where privacy regulations apply (such as the European Economic Area), the App displays a consent form at startup. Users can choose: Personalized ads Non-personalized ads Your choice determines how ads may be tailored. You may also reset your advertising preferences through your device settings. 5. Permissions Used Dhikr+ uses a minimal set of Android permissions. INTERNET Used only to load advertisements from Google AdMob. VIBRATE Used to provide haptic feedback when counting dhikr. The App does not access: Contacts Camera Microphone Files or media Location services Device identifiers beyond what AdMob may collect independently 6. Analytics and Tracking Dhikr+ does not use: Analytics services Crash reporting tools Firebase Tracking SDKs Behavioral analytics The developer does not track user activity inside the app. 7. Data Sharing The developer does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. The only third-party service used is Google AdMob for advertising. Any data collected by AdMob is handled according to Google's own policies. 8. Children's Privacy Dhikr+ is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact the developer so the issue can be addressed. 9. Data Deletion Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time. You can do this by: Clearing the app’s storage through Android system settings, or Uninstalling the App from your device. Once the app data is cleared or the app is removed, all stored information is permanently deleted from your device. 10. Data Security Since Dhikr+ does not collect or transmit personal data, no personal data is stored on external servers. All user data remains locally stored within the app's private storage on the device. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or changes in the App or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. 12. Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, you may contact the developer: Email: glaxier01@gmail.com
